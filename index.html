Imagine if you could...

See better, think more clearly, have improved memory, and even become more intelligent through simple training done on your own computer, smartphone, or tablet. Specialized video game software brings this within reach today by taking advantage of the latest breakthroughs in neuroscience with proven studies that show the brain’s plasticity. Current experimentation continues to confirm that our brain’s function is much more ductile than previously thought and this observation is fueling a mental fitness revolution that is poised to take off. Our Mission

Is to research, test, and disseminate game software instrumented with expert knowledge to optimize human brain processes with an aim to make scientifically principled brain games that translate to performance in real-life activities. Just as physical fitness underwent a revolution in the 20th century, brain fitness is ready to be transformed through innovations in psychology and neuroscience instrumented appropriately through interactive software with modern 3D graphics, artificial intelligence, and computational modeling. Unlocking this potential, the UCR Brain Game Center is creating a new paradigm of evidence based, scientifically optimized brain fitness programs designed to transfer benefits to daily activities in ways that other promising commercial efforts (e.g. Lumosity Labs, Posit Science and Cogmed, etc) have not yet achieves.
